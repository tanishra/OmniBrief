# OmniBrief User Profile — Master Intelligence Configuration

## Core Technical Focus
- **Autonomous & Swarm Agents:** Deep interest in multi-agent orchestration (LangGraph, CrewAI, AutoGen), hierarchical agent architectures, and swarm intelligence.
- **Advanced Agent Memory:** Tracking multi-level memory systems, episodic vs. semantic storage, and long-horizon context management for agents.
- **Orchestration & Frameworks:** High-priority tracking of Pydantic-AI, Swarms, LangGraph, and emerging Python agentic SDKs.
- **Model Context Protocol (MCP):** Full-spectrum monitoring of MCP servers, tools, and standardized interfaces for LLM-to-tool communication.

## Frontier Architectures & Research
- **Inference Efficiency:** Breakthroughs in 1-bit LLMs (BitNet), Mixture-of-Experts (MoE), State Space Models (SSM/Mamba), and quantized local inference.
- **Reasoning Models:** Tracking "Chain of Thought" (CoT) native models (OpenAI o1, DeepSeek-R1) and community replications.
- **A2A & Communication:** Agent-to-Agent communication protocols and autonomous negotiation frameworks.
- **Vision & Multimodal:** Innovative vision-language models (VLM) and spatial reasoning breakthroughs.

## High-Authority Ecosystems
- **Elite Labs:** Always prioritize releases from DeepSeek, Moonshot AI, HKUST (HKDUS), Anthropic (Claude/Antigravity), OpenAI, Microsoft Research, and Google DeepMind.
- **Underdog Innovation:** Identify repos from independent innovators that introduce novel technical primitives rather than simple wrappers.

## Strategic Preferences
- **Python First:** Prioritize Python-based implementations, SDKs, and developer tools.
- **RAG Evolution:** Move beyond basic vector search to GraphRAG, Agentic RAG, and hybrid retrieval systems.
- **Actionability:** Prioritize projects with working code, official implementations, or clear architectural diagrams.

## Noise Filter
- **Ignore:** Low-code/No-code AI wrappers, "Top 50 Prompts" lists, generic marketing SaaS, and art-only generative AI (unless there is a significant architectural breakthrough).
